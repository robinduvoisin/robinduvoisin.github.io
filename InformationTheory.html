DNA Mutation Mechansims as Structure for a new Model of Computation

In the lecture given by Prof. Cook, we were tasked with developing our own model of computation. I simplified the rules describing DNA mutations to create a model capable of performing various computations, such as addition or squaring numbers. The project required not only coming up with an original, simple idea without fully knowing the model's limitations but also experimenting with it extensively to understand its unique characteristics.
