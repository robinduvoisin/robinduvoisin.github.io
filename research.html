Appreciated Literature

Books
  • Gerstner W, Kistler WM, Naud R, Paninski L. (2014). Neuronal Dynamics: From Single Neurons to Networks and Models of Cognition, Cambridge University Press

    • During my Master's program, I was surprised by the absence of similar lectures at ETHZ. Along with a friend from my program, Christian Metzner, we decided to organize our own weekly discussion sessions. We invited six other students to join us in watching the lectures and engaging in conversations afterward. I particularly enjoyed these sessions because we were all at the same level of understanding, and despite not being experts, we had valuable insights to share. I am especially thankful to Wulfram Gerstner for offering all his Neuronal Dynamics Lecture for free online.
